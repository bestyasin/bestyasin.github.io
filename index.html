CURRENT RESEARCH

ABOUT

Currently a postdoctoral scholar in the Posewitz Lab at Colorado School of Mines, engineering P. celeri at the systems level for sustainable bioproduction. Active collaborations with PNNL, LANL, NLR, and EMSL.
